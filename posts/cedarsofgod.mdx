The Cedars of God, known as Arz el-Rab in Lebanon, is a protected grove of ancient Cedar trees nestled in the mountains. 

上帝的雪松（Arz el-Rab）：上帝的雪松在黎巴嫩被称为Arz el-Lab，是一片受保护的古老雪松林，坐落在群山中。

These majestic trees are considered a symbol of Lebanon and have great cultural and historical significance. 

这些雄伟的树木被认为是黎巴嫩的象征，具有重大的文化和历史意义。

Some of the Cedars are estimated to be thousands of years old, and walking through this serene forest provides a sense of tranquility and awe.

据估计，一些雪松已有数千年的历史，穿过这片宁静的森林会给人一种宁静和敬畏的感觉。

The Cedars of God offer scenic hiking trails, a chance to connect with nature, and a glimpse into Lebanon's natural beauty. 

上帝的雪松提供了风景优美的徒步旅行路线，有机会与自然联系，并一瞥黎巴嫩的自然美景。
