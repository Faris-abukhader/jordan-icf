Amman is the capital city of Jordan, located in the northwest of the country. It is a city steeped in history, with evidence of human settlement dating back thousands of years. Today, Amman is a modern metropolis that serves as the political, cultural, and commercial center of Jordan. Its population of over four million people make it the most populous city in the country.
